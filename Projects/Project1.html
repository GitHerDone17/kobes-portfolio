3D-Printed Kinder Toy

Overview

We created a video game inspired Kinder Maxi toy. The toy was based on the Plants Vs. Zombies video game and is referred to as the 'Peashooter'.

It was composed of 5 pieces and could be intuitively assembled/disassembled by kids of all ages. It had a locking mechanism to store potential energy; when released, it launched a small 'pea' over 2 meters with laser precision.

My Role

I led the 3D design portion of the project, utilizing SolidWorks to create and assemble the toy's components. Additionally, I created an animation for showcasing.
