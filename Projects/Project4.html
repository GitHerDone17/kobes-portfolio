Case Study: Autonomous Drone Simulation

Overview

The purpose of the project was to further explore a complex system model, in order to draw relevant conclusions relating to it's capabilites, limitations, and how it can contribute to real-world problem modelling. For this, our group decided to select the Boids algorithm, which was deemed useful in modelling a variety of real world applications.

My Role

My role in the project was to develop the major parts of the code. Provided with the initial library for creating and controlling the Boids, additional functionality was added to the simulation which included bounding the swarms to a 'world', adding obstacles, measuring variance in performance, and more.
